COVID-19 Prevention Research

The search for a coronavirus vaccine is unprecedented, not only in terms of how quickly the industry must pull together to bring one to market but also in the sheer number of volunteers required."

Thank you for your interest in our studies. Science can't move forward without your help!

The purpose of this portal is to create a list of potential volunteers who want to take part in current or future COVID-19 prevention clinical trials. You must be 18 years or older to participate. Our research coordinators will reach out to you to answer your questions and see if you are suitable for any of our active or upcoming studies.

Provide your contact information to receive participation information on COVID-19 prevention studies.

Your information will be used only to contact you regarding Velocity research opportunities. We will not share this information at any time.
