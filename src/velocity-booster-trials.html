Interested in Participating in a Clinical Trial?

Thank you for your interest in clinical research and in finding out more about participating in a COVID-19 Booster study with Velocity. Please fill out the information below and a member of our team will contact you.

All of our studies provide compensation for the time you contribute and any related travel. All treatment and care is provided at no cost, no insurance required. We are always happy to answer any questions you have.

Velocity’s commitment to provide enhanced efficiencies, scalability and productivity while ensuring quality data begins with our carefully vetted research sites. We continually seek dedicated research clinics with highly experienced physician investigators supported by well-trained research staff. Geographical dispersion across the United States ensures patient diversity and access to critical subpopulations.

Our sites have been successfully conducting clinical trials for over 35 years with over 250 years of collective clinical research site operations. All Investigators and site personnel are fully trained and certified in GCP, HIPAA, uniform SOPs and other relevant topics.
