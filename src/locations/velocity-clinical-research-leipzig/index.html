Dr. Sandra Stark joined Velocity Clinical Research after 18 years at Parexel where she held various positions in Project Management, Business Development and Portfolio Management. After leading the Therapeutic Area of Immunology/rare Diseases in Early Phase for 7 years she took on the role of Unit Head of Parexel’s Phase 1 unit in Berlin, Germany, a 120 bed Clinical Pharmacology clinic. Dr. Stark brings 20+ years in both in CRO and biopharmaceutical industry with a broad experience in Early Drug Development, Translational Medicine, Pre-clinical, and thorough strategic development focus on fast and efficient POC to support go/no-go decisions for late-stage development.

Dr. Stark holds a Diploma in Biology from Kassel University and a PhD in Immunology/Immunogenetics from University of Göttingen.

Velocity Clinical Research, Leipzig

Velocity Clinical Research Leipzig, formerly eCovery GmbH, is part of the Velocity Clinical Research Organization, a wholly owned Site Organization, providing excellence in patient care, high quality data and fully integrated research sites.

Our Mission

Velocity’s mission is to bring innovative medical treatments to patients. We are committed to making clinical trials succeed by generating high quality data from as many patients as possible, as quickly as possible while providing exemplary patient care at every step.

Our Story

Conducting Clinical Trials Since 2016

Founded in 2016 as “Sibamed Studienzentrum GmbH & Co. KG”, the Velocity Leipzig team conducts clinical trials in a broad variety of phases and indications, cooperating with specialists in gastroenterology, orthopedics, internal medicine, and pediatrics.

The team focuses on clinical studies without having to look after a turbulent and strenuous conventional practice. Patients appreciate the time the team has for their needs and questions. Even technically challenging hurdles like ePROs, electronic site files, ethic submission or special examinations can be easily managed by the experienced team.

As a reliable partner, Velocity Leipzig is trusted by patients by patients for the care and sponsors for the data, alike. They accept this challenge every day and try to perform it to perfection. The focus is to harmonize the sponsor´s needs with patient´s expectations by always having an open ear to all.

The Velocity Clinical Research, Leipzig team is committed to being a resource for study volunteers, to providing the highest quality of patient care with compassion and kindness and to advancing medicine through research. By becoming part of the Velocity Clinical Research organization in 2022, they added new efficiencies and integration to their already comprehensive approaches to conducting clinical trials.

Our Experienced Research Team

The Velocity Leipzig team of physician investigators and clinical research professionals are experienced clinical researchers. All physicians are board certified in their specialty area(s) and all research staff is trained in clinical research regulations, including GCP and HIPAA. Training records and certifications are maintained. Our clinical team is dedicated to conducting quality research in a professional and caring environment and strives on collecting quality data.
