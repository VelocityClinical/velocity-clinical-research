Sandra joined Velocity Clinical Research after 18 years at Parexel where she held various positions in Project Management, Business Development and Portfolio Management. After leading the Therapeutic Area of Immunology/rare Diseases in Early Phase for 7 years she took on the role of Unit Head of Parexel’s Phase 1 unit in Berlin, Germany, a 120 bed Clinical Pharmacology clinic.

Sandra brings 20+ years in both in CRO and biopharmaceutical industry with a broad experience in Early Drug Development, Translational Medicine, Pre-clinical, and thorough strategic development focus on fast and efficient POC to support go/no-go decisions for late stage development.

Sandra holds a Diploma in Biology from Kassel University and a PhD in Immunology/Immunogenetics from Goettingen University.

Moving from sponsor/CRO to Velocity Clinical not only closes the loop full circle; it expands her experience into late stage development.
