Our Facility is Designed for Clinical Trials

{% for image in page.facility_images %}
{% endfor %}

The San Diego facility is designed for the ease and comfort of study participants and to allow optimal efficiencies in performing clinical trials.  All Velocity sites include:

  • Private Examination/Consultation Rooms
  • Comfortable Patient Reception Areas
  • Facilities for Extended-Stay Pharmacokinetic Studies
  • Parking
  • Nursing Station
  • Secure Monitoring Rooms or Workstations with Phone & High-Speed Internet Access
  • Multiple Individual & Secure Workstations for Research Staff
  • Regulatory Document Processing Area
  • Electronic Data Capture (EDC) Capabilities
  • Secure Record Storage
  • CLIA Certified Research Laboratory or Clinical Laboratory with CLIA waiver
  • Ambient and Refrigerated Centrifuge
  • Secured & Temperature Controlled Investigational Product Storage with Pharmaceutical Refrigerator & Freezer
  • Freezers equipped for laboratory specimens at -20 & -70 degrees Celsius
  • Back Up Generator/ Power Source in the Event of Site Power Loss
  • Standard Medical equipment, including ECG and Holter Machines, digital and analog scales 

In addition, the Velocity Clinical San Diego site has overnight facilities for conducting Phase I studies in multiple therapeutic areas.  This dedicated space includes dormitory style bedrooms with secure storage, lounge area and numerous amenities to provide a comfortable stay for overnight study participants.

Inpatient Facility Capabilities

 

Attentive staff and quality care include:

  • Fresh food and groceries, if allowed per study protocol
  • Cable TV and Netflix in the patient lounge
  • Free WiFi
  • Supervised smoke breaks if allowed per the study protocol
  • Visiting hours daily
  • Around the clock research staff, security, and daily meetings with Nurses and Investigators
  • Follow up visits after a stay with experienced medical clinic staff
  • ACLS certified nurses and BLS certified staff
  • Located next to Sharp Hospitals and ERs

 

Overnight clinical research bedrooms, lounge and amenities include:

  • Several dormitory style bedrooms that are shared for overnight stays
  • Separate rooms for women and men
  • Rooms with separation curtains to enhance comfort and privacy
  • Secure lockers to store personal belongings during stay
  • In addition to a locker, all participants are provided a nightstand beside the bed
  • Private showers and restrooms
  • Laundry available on site
  • Toiletries are provided.  (Study participants may bring own toiletries as well and comfortable clothes)
  • Upon arrival, all study participants receive a box of assorted goodies to welcome you to Velocity San Diego and help make your stay more home-like
