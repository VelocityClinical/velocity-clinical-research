- name: Charles Max Rogers, MD role: Principal Investigator image: bio_html: >-

Born and raised in Mobile, AL, Dr. Max Rogers attended the Virginia Military Institute for his undergraduate degree. After his schooling, he was commissioned in the United States Marine Corps and was designated as a Naval Aviator.

Following his seven years of service in the Marine Corps, Dr. Rogers attended the University of Alabama School of Medicine. Dr. Rogers then headed to the University of Alabama Hospital in Birmingham where he completed his OBGYN residency and internship.

Dr. Rogers has been in private practice since 1998. Dr Rogers worked in Mobile until 2022 at which time he moved North to Grove Hill, Alabama. Finally, Rogers entered the clinical trials as a principal investigator in 2020. Dr. Rogers hopes to continue to bring new solutions to women’s healthcare and other therapeutic areas alike.

Our Story

Conducting Clinical Trials Since 2019

Velocity Clinical Research, Mobile was founded in 2019 as a part of MedPharmics. Since its inception, the site has previously conducted trials in Infectious Disease, Women’s Health, and Pulmonology. They will continue to expand to new therapeutic areas.

The site is easily accessible as it located in Building 2 of the Spring Hill Medical Center, a building filled with a wide variety of doctor’s offices.

The Mobile team is committed to being a resource for study volunteers, to providing the highest quality of patient care with compassion and kindness and to advancing medicine through research. By joining Velocity Clinical Research organization in August 2022, they added new efficiencies and integration to their already comprehensive approaches to conducting clinical trials.

Our Experienced Research Team

The Velocity Mobile team of physician investigators and clinical research professionals are experienced clinical researchers. All physicians are board certified in their specialty area(s) and all research staff is trained in clinical research regulations, including GCP and HIPAA. Training records and certifications are maintained. Our clinical team is dedicated to conducting quality research in a professional and caring environment and strives on collecting quality data.
