Chronic Constipation Research

Constipation is one of the most frequent gastrointestinal complaints in the USA and Western countries. There are at least 2.5 million doctor visits for constipation in the USA each year, and hundreds of millions of dollars are spent on laxatives yearly.

 

Velocity Clinical is currently enrolling for clinical research studies related to Chronic Constipation (CIC).

Qualified participants receive study-related vaccines and exams at no cost and compensation is provided for time and travel. Insurance is not necessary.
