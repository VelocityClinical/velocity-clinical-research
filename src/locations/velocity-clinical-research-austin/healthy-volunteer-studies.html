Celiac Disease Research at Velocity

Velocity is currently enrolling for Celiac Disease studies. 
We conduct studies both investigating the effects of a gluten free diet and working to develop new treatments for celiac disease and gluten sensitivity. As someone with Celiac Disease you can support the advancement of treatments by participating in a research study.
