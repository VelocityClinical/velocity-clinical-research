Know more

Our “DeepCentric” platform is a smart, trusted and secure platform enabling Digital Clinical Trials execution with patient at its center. Hybrid or 100% Decentralized Trials, DeepCentric enriches the Patient-Clinician collaboration, improves the Patient engagement through analytics and instantly digitizes the clinical data by applying deep AI algorithms.

About us

Velocity is committed to improving the clinical research industry in order to bring innovative medical treatments to patients quicker then ever before. Our technology platform is at the core of our ability to advance site-level productivity and radically improve the patient experience.

Velocity’s technology hub in Hyderabad will continue to develop cutting-edge patient facing applications to ease the patient burden when participating in decentralized clinical trials while also increasing the accuracy in which these trials are conducted. Velocity strives to deliver superior accelerated enrollment and consistent reliable results. Our technology platform allows us to continue to do so even as the clinical site landscape changes.
