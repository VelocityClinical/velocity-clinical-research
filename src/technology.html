Technology

Our “DeepCentric” platform is a smart, trusted and secure platform enabling Digital Clinical Trials execution with patient at its center. Hybrid or 100% Decentralized Trials, DeepCentric enriches the Patient-Clinician collaboration, improves the Patient engagement through analytics and instantly digitizes the clinical data by applying deep AI algorithms.

About Us

Velocity is committed to improving the clinical research industry in order to bring innovative medical treatments to patients quicker then ever before. Our technology platform is at the core of our ability to advance site-level productivity and radically improve the patient experience.

Velocity’s technology hub in Hyderabad will continue to develop cutting-edge patient facing applications to ease the patient burden when participating in decentralized clinical trials while also increasing the accuracy in which these trials are conducted. Velocity strives to deliver superior accelerated enrollment and consistent reliable results. Our technology platform allows us to continue to do so even as the clinical site landscape changes.

Our Product

DeepCentric - AI Driven Clinical Trial Platform

Digitize the Sites for Decentralization

Enable sites to perform remote, at home and at site visits through easily portable solution. Using our platform, sites will continue to deliver their critical value by extending their reach.

Patient Engagement through Mobile Analytics

Guide the patient through their daily trial routine to increase their trial adherence. Our deep analytical engine generates actionable insights that are early enough for the sites and Pharma to intervene and engage the patients.

Data Security through Biometrics

Biometric authentication, data encryption and secured conferences to ensure highest level of data security. Our multi-layered security design leaves zero vulnerability to compromise the data security levels.

Flexible clinical work-flows

Work-flows are tailored to the personas. Clinician work-flow was designed with clinician expertise

EDC, CTMS and IVRS Integration

Co-exist with the existing clinical technologies to be instantly inter-operable with our ODM and CDISC compatible solutions

Prioritized Patient Convenience

Every user interaction is designed with patient convenience at its core theme.

Secure Blockchain Platform

All of our clinical data is on a Blockchain platform that delivers data immutability and controlled access. All the integrations are done through Blockchain to ascertain how serious we take trust and auditability

Automation through Edge Technologies

Our custom AI capabilities of Voice, Image, Computer Vision and OCR capabilities offer capabilities that improve the quality and efficiencies of visit conduct and patient-clinician collaborations
