Our History

Velocity Clinical Research, headquartered in Durham, NC, is a wholly owned research site organization focused on patient engagement, quality, scalability and therapeutic breadth. Founded in 2017, Velocity now comprises 12 research sites, geographically dispersed across the United States. Collectively, our sites have been focusing on clinical study trials for over 225 years with more than 4600 successfully completed studies.

The Velocity model represents a new approach to site management. By owning and operating all sites directly, we offer consistent and highly efficient patient recruitment and engagement, and integrated study processes and systems.

Our entire organization focuses on several key principles:

  • Volume and breadth of therapeutic capability
  • Quality experience for both sponsor and patients
  • Consistency and scalability
  • Expedited start-up and rapid patient enrollment
  • Single point of contact for study finances and issue resolution
