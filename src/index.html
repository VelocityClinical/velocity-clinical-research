Who We Are

At Velocity Clinical Research, we have one goal; to deliver high-quality clinical trial data from as many patients as possible, as quickly as possible—providing exemplary patient care at every step.

We're a single organization with multiple locations, not driven by a larger corporation, alliance, or network. We control our own patient care, data, and outcomes. Our work and results are our own.

Support for Sponsors

All site staff, including PIs, are employees of Velocity Clinical Research. We simplify sponsor engagement by providing a central contact for feasibility, contracting, patient recruitment, study management, billing, and payment, whether a sponsor uses one of our sites or all of them.

We are committed to rapid responsiveness both pre- and post-award. Our processes are designed to deliver patients and data as quickly as possible without sacrificing exemplary data quality or patient care.

We work hard to deliver outstanding patient care. It is good for our patients and their families and contributes to the well-being of the communities we serve.
