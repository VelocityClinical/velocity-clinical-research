About Us

An Integrated Approach

Velocity Clinical Research is an owned and integrated research site organization, providing excellence in patient care, high quality data and fully integrated research sites.

Velocity Clinical Research’s integrated approach benefits both Sponsors and study participants. Our model delivers superior accelerated enrollment and consistent, reliable results – helping our clients maintain the highest standards of care while bringing, innovative medical treatments to patients as quickly and efficiently as possible across multiple therapeutic areas.

We are driving improvement to clinical research by enhancing site productivity, integrating study-related technologies and streamlining processes – efficiencies that can only be achieved in a wholly owned site model.
